[Using D3.js to Visualize GIS](http://www.smartjava.org/content/using-d3js-visualize-gis) by Jos Dirksen is a really extensive write-up of his succesful attempt to re-create the [Chloropleth](http://mbostock.github.com/d3/ex/choropleth.html) example from the D3.js website.

He explains how he converted the shape files into PostGIS, exported them to GeoJSON, and used D3.js to show them. I wonder how this could be integrated with [Polymaps](http://polymaps.org/).

More info about D3 geo [here](https://github.com/mbostock/d3/wiki/Geo) and [here](https://github.com/mbostock/d3/wiki/API-Reference).
