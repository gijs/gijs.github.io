Interactivity in webpages is usually limited to simple link clicking and working with form elements, however, let your imagination run off with the idea of 'reactive documents'.

A reactive document, in the context of the web, is a document which enables the reader to play with values, premises and assumptions, and see instant feedback in the same document. [Wolfram Alpha](http://www.wolframalpha.com/input/?i=foot+pound&lk=3) is famous for doing some of this.

[Bret Victor](http://worrydream.com) (check his site out, it's great) explains the concept best in his essay [Explorable Explanations](http://worrydream.com/ExplorableExplanations/), and he has released a Javascript library to create these reactive documents. It's called [Tangle](http://worrydream.com/Tangle/).
