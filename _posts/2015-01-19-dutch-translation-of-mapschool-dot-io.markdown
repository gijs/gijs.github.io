[mapschool.io](http://www.mapschool.io/) is a free online introduction to geo.

![Image](/images/mapschool-gps.jpg "Mapschool.io")

It's the kind of website I wish existed when I started working with GIS. To make it even more accessible for Dutch people and even future colleagues, I decided to contribute to the project by [offering a Dutch translation](https://github.com/tmcw/mapschool/pull/99).

Although translation isn't my speciality, I think it's a fair start and ofcourse anyone is [free to improve it](https://github.com/tmcw/mapschool/fork).

So if you're Dutch and you want to learn more about geo, [take a look](http://mapschool.io/index.nl.html)!
