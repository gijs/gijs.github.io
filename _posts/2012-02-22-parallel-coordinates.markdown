"[Parallel Coordinates](http://eagereyes.org/techniques/parallel-coordinates) are one of the most famous visualization techniques, and among the most common subjects of academic papers in visualization. While initially confusing, they are a very powerful tool for understanding multi-dimensional numerical datasets."
A well executed live example can be viewed and played with at [Parallel Coordinates](http://exposedata.com/parallel/) [exposedata.com], built with [D3.js](http://mbostock.github.com/d3/) and [Backbone.js](http://documentcloud.github.com/backbone/).
