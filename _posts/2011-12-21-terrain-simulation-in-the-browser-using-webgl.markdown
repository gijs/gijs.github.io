Here at [Nelen & Schuurmans](http://www.nelen-schuurmans.nl/), we're doing some really cool 3D simulations like [flooding visualization](http://www.3di.nu/achtergrond_visualisatie/). Wouldn't it be nice to have these applications in the webbrowser, without any need for special equipment or hardware?

I'm convinced that in the very near future, driven by gaming,  [WebGL](http://learningwebgl.com/cookbook/index.php/WebGL:_Frequently_Asked_Questions) will be available in all major webbrowsers and on most platforms. 

This will open the door for 3D simulation like this right in the browser:
