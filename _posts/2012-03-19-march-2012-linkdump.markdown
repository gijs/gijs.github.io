Synchronized Maps with Leaflet and Google Maps
----------------------------------------------

[lxbarth.github.com/compare/](http://lxbarth.github.com/compare/) shows how easy it is to synchronize [Leaflet](http://leaflet.cloudmade.com/) and Google Maps side-by-side. Reminds of [this](http://www.nyholt.nl/blog/2011/11/13/synchronized-panning-and-zooming/).


Vector Layers for Leaflet
-------------------------

[Leaflet Vector Layers](http://geojason.info/leaflet-vector-layers/) is a library for [Leaflet](http://leaflet.cloudmade.com/) that allows management of vector layers. It currently supports ArcGIS Server, Arc2Earth, GeoIQ and CartoDB. (Check out the [CartoDB demo](http://geojason.info/leaflet-vector-layers/demos/cartodb/) or the [PostGIS demo](http://geojason.info/leaflet-vector-layers/demos/postgis-restful-web-service-framework/))

The Open Data Handbook
----------------------
The [Open Data Handbook](http://opendatahandbook.org/en/) discusses the legal, social and technical aspects of open data.  It discusses the why, what and how of open data - why to go open, what open is, and the how to 'open' data.

Someone should fork this idea and make a Dutch version.


Visible Map
-----------
